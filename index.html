About the project

The 3D globe that uses NASA data.
The idea for the web application arose on the hackathon of the NASA Space Apps Challenge.

Whom is our globe for?

For teachers

Teaching geography will be more interactive and you do not need to use paper maps or small images in textbooks. Everything can be shown on the big screen.

For schoolchildren

It is easier for the new generation to assimilate information with the help of technology. Visual stimulation and gamification make learning more interesting.

For parents

This will make it easier for adults to examine the home tasks and memorize the geography lessons themselves 

What can Spheramix do?

  • Hear and seek. The globe hears what you need. Pronounce the city name, landmark, or geographical name. It will hear and show where it is.
  • Zoom in and out. Use commands to zoom in and out. You can even see the city from a height of 100-200 meters.
  • Learn the general and even more. Request a pop-up with brief information about the object. Area, population in countries and cities, language, religion, mountain height, river length, and sea depth – all worth remembering. Also there are the links to follow to the additional information about the object.
